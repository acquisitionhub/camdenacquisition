Do Google reviews actually outperform Google Ads for dental practices?

A practice ranking top 3 organically typically sees higher patient conversion rates than paid ad traffic, because the patient has already chosen to search for a specific type of dentist in a specific area before clicking.
What about using both paid ads and Google reviews together?
Many well-resourced practices do both. Paid ads deliver immediate volume; reviews build long-term organic ranking. If budget allows, the combination is strong. But for most independent NW London practices, the organic approach alone is sufficient — and more cost-effective when measured by cost per booked patient.
