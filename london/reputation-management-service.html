Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Which platforms do you monitor?

We monitor all the platforms that matter for London dental practices — Google Business Profile, NHS Choices, Trustpilot, and others. If a patient is writing about you online, we'll know about it.

What happens when a negative review is posted?

You'll get an instant alert with the review details and our suggested response. For future reviews, our system tries to identify unhappy patients before they post publicly — routing them to a private feedback form so you can resolve the issue first.

Can you remove negative reviews?

No one can guarantee removal of reviews — and anyone who claims otherwise isn't being straight with you. What we can do is help you respond professionally, reduce the flow of negative reviews through private routing, and build enough positive volume that one bad review doesn't define your rating.

How long before I see a difference in my ratings?

Review volume typically starts climbing within the first week. Star rating improvements depend on your starting point — but most practices see a meaningful improvement within 60–90 days as positive reviews outpace any negatives.
