Why Timing Is Everything in Review Collection

A patient who just had a good appointment is in the ideal mental state to leave a review — grateful, satisfied, and with the experience fresh in their mind. Ask them three days later and the moment has passed. Ask them via a laminated card at reception and they'll forget before they reach the car park.

Post-appointment SMS review requests work because they arrive at exactly the right moment — within minutes of the appointment, on the device the patient already has in their hand. One tap takes them directly to your Google review form. No searching, no copying a link, no remembering to do it later.

For NW1 and NW3 practices, this timing advantage compounds over weeks and months — building a consistent weekly flow of new reviews that steadily improves your Map Pack ranking for Camden and Hampstead searches.
