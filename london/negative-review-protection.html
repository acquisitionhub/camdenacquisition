How Negative Review Protection Works in Practice

Every review request sent by our system includes a simple satisfaction check before directing the patient to Google. Patients who indicate a poor experience are routed to a private feedback form — where you can address the issue directly — instead of your public Google profile. Satisfied patients go straight to Google to leave their 5-star review.

This two-path system means the patients most likely to leave negative public reviews are intercepted privately, while happy patients flow directly to your Google Business Profile. Over time, this builds a large volume of positive reviews while keeping negative experiences offline and manageable.

Meanwhile, 24/7 monitoring across Google, Yelp, and Facebook means that if a review does land — whether through our system or directly — you know within minutes, not days.
