The Hidden Revenue in Your Lapsed Patient List

Most Camden and Hampstead dental practices focus entirely on attracting new patients — but they're sitting on a pool of lapsed patients who are significantly easier and cheaper to convert. These are people who visited your practice, had a positive enough experience to return, then life got in the way. They didn't leave because of a bad experience. They just drifted.

Re-engaging a lapsed patient requires far less trust-building than acquiring a new one. They know your practice, your location, and broadly what to expect. A personalised, timely SMS with a booking link is often all it takes to bring them back — especially when combined with a strong review profile that reminds them why they chose you originally.

Our lost patient recovery service works by systematically reaching your lapsed patient list — gradually and naturally, so it doesn't create an artificial spike — and delivering your booking link directly. The patients who respond are already warm. They just needed a prompt.
