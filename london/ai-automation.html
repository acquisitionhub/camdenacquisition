FAQ

Common Questions About the AI Automation Suite

Is this different from the Missed Call Recovery service?

Missed Call Recovery covers one leak point: unanswered phone calls. The AI Automation Suite extends the same instant-response principle to web enquiries, then adds treatment plan follow-up, lapsed patient reactivation, and weekly practice reporting — covering every stage where a patient or piece of revenue can quietly disappear.

Will this work with my existing practice management software?

Yes. These automations are designed to sit alongside whatever practice management or booking software you already use. Nothing is replaced — the automation layer simply connects to your enquiry channels, patient list, and diary.

How long does it take to go live?

Each automation typically goes live within one to two weeks of setup. You can start with a single automation — most practices begin with the Lead Response Engine — and add the others as needed.

Is patient data handled securely?

Yes. All automations are run to the same UK GDPR compliant standard as the rest of Patient Acquisition Hub's services. Patient data is never sold or shared with third parties, and full guidance on consent is provided during onboarding.

Can I add this to my existing Patient Acquisition Hub plan?

Yes. Each automation can be added individually to an existing engagement, or combined with the New Patient Portal as the New Patient Portal Pro bundle for practices that want the full system in one go.
