How Does the Missed Call Revenue Calculator Work?

This free tool helps UK dental practice owners understand the real financial cost of unanswered patient calls. It uses one straightforward formula:

Monthly Lost Revenue = Missed Calls Per Day × Average Patient Value (£) × 30

For example: a dental practice missing 5 calls per day at an average patient value of £300 loses £45,000 every month — or £540,000 per year. These are patients who called, found no one, and booked with a competitor instead.

Why Do Dental Practices Lose Patients from Missed Calls?

Most dental practices don't answer every call. Calls go unanswered during busy clinic sessions, lunch breaks, when reception staff are with patients, and after opening hours. Research consistently shows that patients who can't reach a practice on the first attempt rarely try again — they simply book with the next available dentist. Every unanswered call is a decision point, and most patients don't wait.

How Many Calls Does the Average UK Dental Practice Miss?

Industry estimates suggest UK dental practices miss between 3 and 8 calls per day on average, with the number rising significantly during peak periods. Even at the lower end of 3 missed calls per day and a patient value of £200, that's £18,000 in lost monthly revenue — or over £216,000 a year.

What Is the Average Patient Value for a UK Dental Practice?

Patient value varies considerably by practice type. Private dental practices typically see new patient values of £150–£500 per course of treatment. Specialist practices focused on implants, orthodontics, or cosmetic dentistry often see average treatment values of £1,000 or more. NHS practices can use their average band charge or course-of-treatment value in the calculator above.

How Can Dental Practices Stop Losing Revenue from Missed Calls?

The most effective solutions include AI-powered answering systems that handle calls around the clock, automated SMS follow-up for missed callers, overflow answering services for peak hours, and integrated online booking. The best tools re-engage missed callers within minutes and convert them into booked appointments — without adding headcount to your reception team.
