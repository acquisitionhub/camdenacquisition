Why Automated Review Requests Beat Manual Every Time

The average Camden or Hampstead dental practice collects 2 to 3 Google reviews per month — a laminated card at reception, a vague mention at checkout. The top-ranked NW1 and NW3 practices have 150 to 300 reviews. The difference isn't better dentistry. It's a system.

Manual review requests fail because they depend on a busy reception team remembering to ask, patients remembering to act, and the timing being right. Automated review requests solve all three — sending a personalised message within minutes of the appointment, directly to the patient's phone, with one tap to their Google review form. No friction, no forgetting, no chasing.

Our automated patient review request system is powered by Try Review Bloom — the review automation product built specifically for dental practices. It runs in the background from day one, collecting reviews consistently without any effort from your team.

How the Automation Works

1

Appointment triggers the request

When a patient's appointment ends — via your PMS or a manual trigger — the system automatically queues a personalised review request. No action needed from your reception team.

2

SMS sent within minutes

A personalised SMS arrives on the patient's phone while the experience is still fresh. It includes their name, a friendly message, and a direct link to your Google Business Profile review page — one tap to leave a review.

3

Geo-keyword prompt included

The message gently encourages patients to mention their area — Camden, Hampstead, NW1, NW3 — in their review text. These geo-keywords in review content are a direct local ranking signal for Map Pack positions.

4

Email follow-up if no response

Patients who don't respond to the SMS receive a polite email follow-up. This significantly increases total review conversion without any additional effort or awkward chasing from your team.
