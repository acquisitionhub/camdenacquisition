Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will we recover our first missed-call patient?

Most practices recover their first missed-call patient within 24–48 hours of going live. The system starts working from the moment it's switched on — there's no warm-up period needed.

Do we need to change our phone system?

No. Our system works alongside your existing phone line — no new hardware, no complicated setup. We do the technical work during onboarding and your team won't notice any change in how calls come in.

What if a patient doesn't want an SMS?

All messages include an easy opt-out option and are fully compliant with UK GDPR. Patients can unsubscribe from future messages with a single reply.

Does it work after hours and on weekends?

Absolutely — this is actually where it's most valuable. London patients often call in the evening or on weekends when they have time to think about booking. Our system catches those calls 24/7.

Can we see which calls converted into bookings?

Yes. Your dashboard shows every missed call, when the SMS was sent, and whether a booking was made — giving you a clear picture of your return on investment.
